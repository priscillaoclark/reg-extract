EXPORT-IMPORT BANK


Intent To Conduct a Detailed Economic Impact Analysis

AGENCY: Export-Import Bank.

ACTION: Notice.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY: Pursuant to the Charter of the Export-Import Bank of the 
United States, this notice is to inform the public that the Export-
Import Bank of the United States has received an application for $39.8 
million in medium-term insurance to support the export of approximately 
$45.7 million worth of U.S. aluminum beverage cans and ends 
manufacturing equipment to Brazil. The U.S. exports will enable the 
Brazilian company to expand its existing production by 3 billion 
aluminum cans per year and 2.8 billion aluminum can ends per year. New 
production will be sold in Brazil.

DATES: Comments are due 14 days from publication in the Federal 
Register.

ADDRESSES: Interested parties may submit comments on this transaction 
electronically on www.regulations.gov, or by email to 
[email protected].
